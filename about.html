OUR HERITAGE

Rooted in Tradition, Crafted for Eternity

For over 25 years, Mist Aoud has been crafting extraordinary fragrances that honor the ancient art of oud perfumery. Our journey began in the heart of the Middle East, where master perfumers taught us the sacred traditions of scent creation.

Today, we continue to source the finest oud from sustainable forests, ensuring each bottle carries not just a fragrance, but a piece of history. Our commitment to authenticity and quality has made us a trusted name among fragrance connoisseurs worldwide.
